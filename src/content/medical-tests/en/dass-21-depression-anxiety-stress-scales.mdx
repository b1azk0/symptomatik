    body: "Components of anxiety and stress encompass interrelated domains clinicians assess to guide care: physiological signs (sleep disturbance, autonomic arousal, somatic complaints), cognitive features (excessive worry, catastrophizing, concentration problems), emotional symptoms (fear, irritability, low mood), and behavioral responses (avoidance, safety behaviors, substance use).
